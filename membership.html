Frequently Asked Questions

How do I activate my membership?

After submitting your application and payment, your membership will be activated immediately. You'll receive a confirmation email with your membership details.

Can I upgrade my membership plan?

Yes! You can upgrade your membership at any time. The difference in price will be calculated on a pro-rata basis.

Are membership benefits transferable?

No, membership benefits are non-transferable and can only be used by the registered member.

What happens if I miss my free monthly service?

Free monthly services must be used within the calendar month. They cannot be carried forward to the next month.

Can I cancel my membership?

Memberships are annual and non-refundable. However, you can choose not to renew at the end of your term.

Do members get additional discounts on offers?

Membership discounts cannot be combined with other promotional offers. You'll always receive the better of the two discounts.
