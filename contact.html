Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to book an appointment in advance?

While walk-ins are welcome, we highly recommend booking an appointment in advance to ensure availability and avoid waiting times.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, credit/debit cards, UPI, and digital wallets. We also accept payment through our membership packages.

How early should I arrive for my appointment?

We recommend arriving 10-15 minutes before your scheduled appointment to allow time for check-in and preparation.

Do you offer group packages or corporate discounts?

Yes, we offer special packages for groups and corporate clients. Please contact us directly for customized packages and pricing.

What should I wear for my spa treatment?

We provide disposable garments and robes for most treatments. Wear comfortable clothing to the spa. For specific treatments like waxing, you may need to remove certain clothing items.

Are your products safe for sensitive skin?

Yes, we use high-quality, natural, and hypoallergenic products. However, please inform our staff about any skin sensitivities or allergies before your treatment.
